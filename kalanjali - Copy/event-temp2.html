FASHION SHOW

  1. One team per college.
  2. Minimum number of participants per team is 10 and maximum is 14.
    In addition there may be 1 person in charge of music and 1 person for lights.
  3. Stage time for each team is limited to a maximum of 8+2 mins. 2 mins for stage setup.
  4. Only one round is allowed.
  5. Teams will be judged on creativity, adherence to the theme, styling, choreography and music.
  6. Any form of obscenity will result in debarring the team from further participation.
  7. Registration fees: 1000/- per team
